# Privacy Policy for zBrowser

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Abdullah Al Amin ("we", "our", or "us") operates the zBrowser mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Application.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
zBrowser is designed as a privacy-focused web browser. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data, browsing history, or search queries. All browsing data, tabs, and shortcuts remain locally on your device.

## 2. Permissions & Third-Party Services
To provide core browser functionality, the Application utilizes the following permissions and external implementations:
* **Internet Permission:** Required to load external websites, render web views, and fetch data.
* **WebView Component:** Displays standard web content. External websites visited via zBrowser may collect data or use cookies subject to their own independent privacy policies.
* **Discover Feed API:** Utilizes standard network requests to fetch current news and content.

## 3. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
